different

(But Thats Okay)

HTML 5

The Content

All that is or ever will be must go through HTML. While that might be a stretch none of the other languages would have anything to work with without some good old HTML. Unless your site is written in php it will be writen in HTML.

CSS 3

The Look

CSS is the way that your website looks. It is the formating, the design. Without CSS your website is just words. Not even very pretty words either.

jQuery

The Animator

When you see something move on a website you can be sure that it's some form of javascript. And using the jQuery library is a fast way to get good results.

PHP and MySQL

The Communication Systems

PHP can be compared to jQuery in that it does stuff, but where jQuery tells your browser to do something neat, PHP works with your website. It retrieves data from your database, allowing more meaningful actions to take place.

I work for you!

Besides the ability to create or modify your website I also offer other, related services.

Photography

Along with basic photoshop skills for handling your photos, I can aquire stock photos for the site if you don't have any images of your own. If you have a design template you would like to use I can also quickly convert your photoshop files into the needed html and css for your website.

Wordpress Themes

Hosting Services

Have you ever had to deal with a cable company? Well, hosting companies are a lot like that. For a monthly fee I can hook you up with hosting and/or a domain name and keep your site hosted. If your site runs on Wordpress I will also keep your installation and plugins up to date.
